## Active principles

- Shape the environment so required verification is structural, while preserving judgment and ordinary direct work.
- Escalate progressively from the smallest sufficient environment: activate controls from concrete hazards, not task size or a named-mode ceremony, and fast-path directly to a relevant control when its trigger is already known.
- Keep the persistent parent context lean through an artifact-backed coordination index only when the task has a real need for it.
- Treat provenance and evidence class as task-local claims; stop dependent work when required evidence is missing or mismatched.
- Preserve transactional artifact safety, independent review, protected boundaries, and full authorized source access.
- Use receipts, joins, and repair handoffs conditionally where they pay for themselves; do not create universal ledgers or schemas.
- Treat repeated or metered consequential execution as a renewable lease around the scarce operation, with a proving unit, observable accounting, operational stop signals, and explicit renewal.
- Make lease renewal decision-aware: distinguish operational canaries from decision pilots, order prerequisites before dependent work, and inspect health, spend, and whether more units can still change the current decision.
- Treat warm continuation as an optional same-unit experiment, never as a reviewer or promotion shortcut.
- Distinguish runtime telemetry from billing, unique-token waste, and causal savings.
- Challenge instrument discrimination before promoting a load-bearing empirical interpretation; arithmetic agreement alone does not establish informativeness.

## Decisions taken

- Generalize evidence gates and receipt semantics instead of hard-coding campaign event names or evidence enums.
- Keep detailed campaign experiments in campaign documentation or harness tests, not in the general skill.
- Keep exact-answer verification in the existing closeout/final-gate path and trigger extra comparison only when synthesis changes load-bearing claims.
- Keep warm-context policy in the subagent brief, where the actual warm-context contract lives, rather than duplicating it in `SKILL.md`.
- Treat audit or efficiency wording alone as insufficient to activate receipt machinery; comparative or joinable execution evidence must actually be needed.
- Trigger execution leases only for repeated work with material cost, duration, capacity, mutation, or decision-relevant partial health evidence; preserve the direct path for cheap loops and ordinary tests.
- Put external budgets around the scarce operation rather than assuming a subagent's file or attempt budget constrains what it can launch.
- Keep empirical instrument challenges conditional on load-bearing evidence decisions, not as a universal review checklist.
- Keep decision-frontier and result-stop fields conditional on dependent branches or lease-triggered execution; do not add them to ordinary direct work.

## Editing considerations recorded

- Source basis: the operative skill and its nearest reference are the evidence base for an edit.
- Framing basis: a mistaken working frame and the intended reflex are identified before new wording is considered.
- Economy basis: compact wording is preferred over new checklists or mandatory artifacts.
- Scope basis: heavy coordination machinery is limited to asynchronous delegation, dependent promotion, protected-boundary risk, or an actual need for comparative or joinable execution evidence.
- Direct-path basis: clear, local, reversible work remains eligible for ordinary handling.
- Reasoning basis: source access is preserved; persistence of hidden/internal reasoning is not a requirement.
- Integrity basis: prior verdicts and accepted artifacts remain immutable; repairs are superseding changes.
- Validation basis: the motivating failure, a normal complex task, and an over-ceremony opposite case are the forward-test surfaces.
